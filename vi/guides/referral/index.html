Huong dan tung buoc

Chuong trinh gioi thieu nhan vien

Gioi thieu dong nghiep su dung Rakuten Mobile va ca hai deu nhan duoc diem thuong Rakuten. Huong dan nay se chi ban qua trinh gioi thieu voi anh chup man hinh.

Tong quan chuong trinh

Nguoi gioi thieu

Ban nhan diem thuong cho moi lan gioi thieu thanh cong

Nguoi duoc gioi thieu

Dong nghiep cua ban cung nhan diem thuong khi dang ky

Dieu kien:
  • Ca nguoi gioi thieu va nguoi duoc gioi thieu deu phai la nhan vien cua cong ty du dieu kien
  • Nguoi duoc gioi thieu phai la thue bao moi cua Rakuten Mobile
  • Lien ket/ma gioi thieu phai duoc su dung tai thoi diem dang ky
1

Truy cap trang chuong trinh gioi thieu

Dang nhap vao tai khoan Rakuten va di den trang chuong trinh gioi thieu nhan vien. Trang nay thuong duoc tim thay qua cong thong tin noi bo cua cong ty hoac lien ket truc tiep tu nha tuyen dung.

Anh chup man hinh: Trang chu chuong trinh gioi thieu

2

Lay lien ket hoac ma gioi thieu

Tren trang gioi thieu, ban se tim thay lien ket hoac ma gioi thieu rieng cua minh. Sao chep lien ket nay - ban se chia se no voi dong nghiep. Moi lien ket gioi thieu duoc gan voi tai khoan Rakuten cua ban.

Anh chup man hinh: Trang tao lien ket/ma gioi thieu
Add image to: assets/guides/referral-step2.png
Meo: Luu lien ket gioi thieu o noi tien loi (ung dung Ghi chu, danh dau trang, v.v.) de ban co the chia se bat cu luc nao dong nghiep hoi ve Rakuten Mobile.
3

Chia se lien ket voi dong nghiep

Gui lien ket gioi thieu cho dong nghiep qua email, LINE, Slack hoac bat ky ung dung nhan tin nao. Dam bao ho hieu rang can su dung lien ket cu the nay khi dang ky Rakuten Mobile.

Anh chup man hinh: Chia se lien ket gioi thieu
Add image to: assets/guides/referral-step3.png
4

Dong nghiep dang ky qua lien ket cua ban

Dong nghiep nhan vao lien ket gioi thieu, se duoc chuyen den trang dang ky Rakuten Mobile. Ho hoan thanh quy trinh dang ky binh thuong. Ma gioi thieu se tu dong duoc ap dung khi su dung lien ket cua ban.

Anh chup man hinh: Trang dang ky Rakuten Mobile qua lien ket gioi thieu
Add image to: assets/guides/referral-step4.png
Quan trong: Neu dong nghiep roi khoi lien ket gioi thieu va truy cap truc tiep Rakuten Mobile, gioi thieu co the khong duoc theo doi. Dam bao ho su dung lien ket cua ban de bat dau dang ky.
5

Dong nghiep tao tai khoan Rakuten (neu can)

Neu dong nghiep chua co tai khoan Rakuten, ho can tao mot tai khoan truoc. Huong dan ho tham khao Huong dan dang ky tai khoan Rakuten de biet cac buoc chi tiet.

Anh chup man hinh: Tao tai khoan Rakuten trong quy trinh gioi thieu

6

Hoan thanh dang ky va kich hoat

Dong nghiep hoan thanh dang ky Rakuten Mobile, nhan the SIM va kich hoat dich vu. Thuong gioi thieu thuong duoc xu ly sau khi duong day moi duoc kich hoat va hoat dong trong thoi gian du dieu kien.

Anh chup man hinh: Xac nhan hoan thanh dang ky
Add image to: assets/guides/referral-step6.png
7

Nhan diem thuong

Sau khi duong day cua dong nghiep duoc kich hoat va qua thoi gian du dieu kien, ca hai se nhan duoc diem thuong Rakuten. Ban co the kiem tra trang thai trong tai khoan Rakuten tai trang chuong trinh gioi thieu.

  • Diem thuong thuong duoc cong trong vong 1-2 thang sau khi kich hoat
  • Kiem tra Rakuten Point Club de xem diem thuong
  • Diem co the su dung tren tat ca dich vu Rakuten

Cau hoi thuong gap

Toi co the gioi thieu bao nhieu nguoi?
Thuong khong co gioi han so luong gioi thieu, nhung hay kiem tra dieu khoan chuong trinh cu the cua cong ty ban de biet co gioi han nao khong.
Toi co the gioi thieu nguoi da co Rakuten Mobile khong?
Khong, nguoi duoc gioi thieu phai la thue bao moi cua Rakuten Mobile. Thue bao hien tai khong du dieu kien.
Neu dong nghiep huy sau khi dang ky thi sao?
Neu nguoi duoc gioi thieu huy truoc thoi gian du dieu kien, diem thuong co the bi thu hoi hoac khong duoc cap. Dich vu phai duy tri hoat dong trong thoi gian quy dinh.
Diem thuong co het han khong?
Diem thuong gioi thieu co the la "diem co thoi han" (期間限定ポイント) voi ngay het han. Kiem tra chi tiet diem trong Rakuten Point Club cua ban.
