Step-by-Step Guide

How to Register a Rakuten Account

A Rakuten account (楽天ID) is required for Rakuten Mobile, Rakuten Card, Rakuten Ichiba shopping, and all Rakuten services. Registration takes about 5 minutes and is free.

Before you start

You will need:
  • An email address
  • A phone number (for verification)
  • About 5 minutes
Good to know:
  • You can switch the page language using the Language button at the bottom
  • Screenshots below show the English interface
  • The process is the same regardless of language
1

Go to the Rakuten login page and click "Create a new Rakuten account"

Visit any Rakuten service login page. At the bottom, click the pink "Create a new Rakuten account (free)" button to start registration.

2

Enter your email address

On the registration page, enter your email address in the "Email (Required)" field. This email will be used to sign in to your account. Rakuten will send a verification code to this address. By registering, you agree to the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy. Click "Send verification code" to proceed.

3

Enter the verification code

Rakuten sends a 6-digit verification code to your email. Check your inbox, find the code, and enter it in the "Verification code (Required)" field. The code is valid for 10 minutes. Click "Verify email address" to continue.

Didn't receive the code? Check your spam/junk folder. You can click "Resend verification code" after the countdown timer expires. If you entered the wrong email, click "Edit email address" to go back and correct it.
4

Set your username, password, and name

After verification, fill in your account details:

  • Username - defaults to your email. Check "Use my email as my username" to keep it simple
  • Password - create a strong password (aim for "Medium" strength or higher)
  • Re-enter password - type it again to confirm
  • First name / Last name - enter your name as it appears on your ID

Click "Continue to confirmation page" when done.

Tip for foreigners: Use the name exactly as it appears on your residence card (在留カード). The English interface accepts roman characters directly.
5

Confirm your information and complete registration

Review all your details on the confirmation page: email, username, first name, and last name. If everything looks correct, click the pink "Complete registration" button. If you need to fix anything, click "Edit your information" to go back.

6

Registration complete!

Your Rakuten account is now ready. You can use this account for all Rakuten services:

  • Rakuten Mobile - sign up for a phone plan
  • Rakuten Card - apply for a credit card
  • Rakuten Ichiba - online shopping
  • Rakuten Pay - cashless payments
  • Rakuten Points - earn and spend across all services
